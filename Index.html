WELCOME TO PEOPLE VS COFFEE

A pop-up espresso cafe offering an amazing coffee experience and friendly baristas in the slickest locations
around the Barossa, Adelaide, and surrounding locations. We are available for weddings, private and
corporate events, sporting gatherings, festivals, and local markets.

POP-UP CAFE

Our coffee van event servers will create an authentic experience and ensure your guests will be well caffeinated and full of delicious treats. Services include cafe services, fully trained baristas, and a divine offering of croissants and pastries.

ESPRESSO BAR

Our Espresso bar is an unforgettable experience offering espresso martinis,
champagne, and other cocktails as required. Our fully trained bar staff will ensure your guests are ready to celebrate well into the evening.

OUR MISSION

WE KEEP IT LOCAL & SUSTAINABLE

We want you to enjoy your coffee experience with us. For this reason, we source the finest local
ingredients, from our locally roasted beans by Bean Addiction to our milk produced by Jersey
Fresh. All our offerings are served in sustainable packaging to ensure we don't leave a footprint
behind. We pop-up and pop-out cleanly.

OUR PROMISE TO GIVE

We guarantee: You'll love it or your money back!
We love living and working in the Barossa Valley and want to give back to the community, so we promise to donate
20c from every coffee we sell to local charities.
